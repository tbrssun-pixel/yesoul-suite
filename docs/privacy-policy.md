# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-11

Owl Bike V1 Tracker is a local-first Android app for cycling workout tracking and BLE diagnostics on compatible exercise bikes.

## Data The App Handles

The app can store the following data locally on your device:

- workout sessions and metric samples;
- bike telemetry shown during a workout, such as speed, cadence, power, heart rate, distance, calories, and resistance/load when exposed by the bike;
- BLE diagnostic snapshots used to understand device compatibility.

## Data Collection And Sharing

The developer does not collect, receive, sell, or share your app data.

The app does not use:

- accounts;
- cloud sync;
- advertising;
- analytics;
- crash reporting;
- subscriptions or payments.

Workout exports and diagnostics are shared only when you choose to share them through Android's system share sheet. Diagnostics hide the Bluetooth device address before sharing.

## Permissions

The app requests Bluetooth permissions to find and connect to a compatible bike. On Android 11 and lower, Android requires foreground location permission for BLE scanning. The app does not record location coordinates and does not request background location.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Workout history and diagnostics remain on your device until you delete the app or clear its app data through Android settings. Android backup for the app database is disabled.

## Health Disclaimer

Owl Bike V1 Tracker is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition and does not provide medical advice.

## Brand Disclaimer

Owl Bike V1 Tracker is compatible with YESOUL Bike V1 / YS-003 testing data, but it is not an official YESOUL app and is not affiliated with YESOUL.
